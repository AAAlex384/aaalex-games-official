TEAMS

A list of all the teams AAAlex Games contains

AAAlex Games

The main team that acts more of an umbrella category. It also serves as the general team if a project does not belong to any specific sub-team.

Uranian Productions (UP)

A sub-team that actually predates AAAlex Games all together. The sub-team works solely on Crontike related projects, which is an original series with plothole-less worldbuilding and stories.

Eclipsed Studio

A sub-team that specializes in fan projects of any sort. The co-founder is "Xarika!" on the Discord, and they help manage projects.
