The AAAlex Games Official Website is here, and this is our first Newsletter! All thanks to LastieOS for coding the site in Github. Feel free to donate to us for support. Anyway, let's get started with our Newsletter!

Hydia: The Hidden World

Hydia: The Hidden World's production has been going... slow, it's been slow. Like, it hasn't been touched since December of 2025. But, we can say that there is a basic movement script and other sprites. Meanwhile, we have no text system, no battle system, and nothing else other than music and a few art assets. But, over the upcoming summer, we plan on putting production into overdrive! In the meantime, we'll try to work when we can. But, we have hopes to have Chapter 1 done, or at least close to done, before the end of 2026. Either way, we're very excited to work some more on the project.

Space Flight

Space Flight was a game posted on our Itch page back in July of 2025. Currently, there was an idea to make a remixed version with more content. So, this project is the back of the documents as it's not our priority.

New Member!

We have a new member to our team: LastieOS! The programmer for our site on Github, who has been a big help.
